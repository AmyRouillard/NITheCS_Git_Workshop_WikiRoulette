Hawley Beach is a rural and residential locality in the local government area (LGA) of Latrobe in the north-west and west region of Tasmania. 
The locality is about 22 kilometres (14 mi) north-east of the town of Latrobe. The 2016 census has a population of 484 for the state suburb of Hawley Beach.[1] It is a locality and seaside resort town 22 kilometres (14 mi) from the nearest main town (Devonport). 
Nearby is the small town of Port Sorell and locality of Shearwater.
